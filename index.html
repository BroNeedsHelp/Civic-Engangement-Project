Sized Tax Reduction Incentives for Vote Extraction

What is STRIVE?

STRIVE (Sized Tax Reduction Incentives for Vote Extraction) is a proposed initiative focused on optimizing tax incentives through strategic civic participation. It aims to align fiscal reductions with verified voting engagement, promoting both economic and democratic health.

Why STRIVE?

Eligibility Requirements

Participants must be registered voters and provide proof of voting in local, state, or federal elections. STRIVE also includes education on responsible voting practices and data transparency protocols.
